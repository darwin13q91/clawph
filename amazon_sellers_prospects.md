## Key Pain Points Already Expressed by These Sellers (ICP Match Signals)

- Sellers crossing **$10k/mo report** → shift from "hustle" to needing **systems, automation, proper finance setup**
- Multiple sellers explicitly **hired VAs** to handle catalog management, customer service, product research (perfect for Amajungle's AI support offering)
- Sellers at **$50k–$100k/mo** are drowning in PPC management, inventory, returns — prime for Amajungle's $997+$999/mo full-service offer
- Reddit threads on "is it possible to scale on Amazon without a team?" — strong signal of need for outsourced automation

## Notes for Outreach

- **Best warm intro angle:** "I saw your case study / your story on [podcast/YouTube] — we're working with sellers like you who are past the $10k/mo mark and need to systematize before they can scale to $50k+"
