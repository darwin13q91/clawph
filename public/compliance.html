⚠️ What We Don't Do

We don't log into your Seller Central account.

Every week a new tool promises "AI that runs your Amazon business hands-free." Here's the truth: any tool that logs into Seller Central, clicks around, creates cases, or makes changes on your behalf is violating Amazon's Terms of Service.

If you use these tools, you're risking account suspension. We've seen sellers lose years of business overnight because they trusted the wrong automation.

Common Questions

Q: Can you fix my suppressed listing? A: We can identify why it's suppressed and tell you exactly how to fix it. You (or your team) make the changes in Seller Central.
Q: Can you automatically adjust my prices? A: No. We monitor pricing and alert you to opportunities. You decide whether to adjust.
Q: Can you create cases in Seller Central for me? A: No. We can help you draft the case text and guide you through the process, but you submit it.
Q: Is this really compliant with Amazon TOS? A: Yes. We only use approved APIs and never interact with Seller Central's interface. Your account stays 100% under your control.
