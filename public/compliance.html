# Compliance & Transparency ## What We Don't Do (And Why) **We don't log into your Seller Central account.** Every week a new tool promises "AI that runs your Amazon business hands-free." Here's the truth: **any tool that logs into Seller Central, clicks around, creates cases, or makes changes on your behalf is violating Amazon's Terms of Service.** Amazon explicitly prohibits: - Automated browser tools that mimic human behavior - Bots that navigate Seller Central - Frameworks that interact with Amazon's interface programmatically - Any "hands-free" automation inside your account **If you use these tools, you're risking account suspension.** We've seen sellers lose years of business overnight because they trusted the wrong automation. --- ## What We Actually Do **We analyze. You decide. You execute.** We don't touch your Seller Central account. Instead, we: ### 1. Intelligence Gathering - Analyze your listings, reviews, and competitor data - Monitor account health metrics via Amazon's official APIs - Track Buy Box status and pricing trends - Audit your catalog for optimization opportunities ### 2. Strategic Recommendations - Get specific, actionable insights ("Your main image is losing clicks to Competitor X - here's why") - Receive optimization suggestions that comply with Amazon's guidelines - Understand exactly what's hurting your conversion rate ### 3. You Stay in Control - You review every recommendation - You make the changes in Seller Central (or delegate to your team) - You maintain full ownership and accountability **Think of us as your Amazon intelligence department, not your automation bot.** --- ## How We Access Data (The Technical Stuff) ### Official APIs Only We use Amazon's approved APIs (SP-API) to pull data: - Order reports - Inventory levels - Listing information - Account health metrics **We never:** - Screen-scrape Seller Central - Use browser automation - Store your login credentials - Make changes on your behalf ### Data Security - Your data stays encrypted - We don't share or sell your information - You can request data deletion anytime - Full GDPR compliance --- ## The Bottom Line **Amazon compliance isn't optional — it's the foundation of a sustainable business.** The sellers who win long-term are the ones who: - Understand Amazon's rules - Build systems that work within those rules - Use intelligence, not automation, to make better decisions **We're here to help you do exactly that.** --- ## Questions? **Q: Can you fix my suppressed listing?** A: We can identify why it's suppressed and tell you exactly how to fix it.
