## The Protocol

### 1. CAPTURE
Save raw input exactly as given. Never discard.
**Location:** `/memory/brain-dumps/YYYY-MM-DD_HHMMSS_{id}.md`

### 2. CATEGORIZE
Auto-tag with categories:
- **Ideas** — New concepts, opportunities
- **Tasks** — Action items, to-dos
- **Research** — Links, articles, findings
- **Meetings** — Notes, decisions, follow-ups
- **Goals** — Objectives, targets
- **Random** — Everything else
- **Amajungle** — Business-specific

### 3. SUMMARIZE
Distill to 1-2 sentences. Store alongside raw.

### 4. CONNECT
Flag connections to previous notes:
> "This connects to [2026-03-10: Idea about X]"

### 5. SURFACE
On query "what have I said about X?" — search and rank.
